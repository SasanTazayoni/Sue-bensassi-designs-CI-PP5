Delivery Times

We make every effort to ensure that you receive your delivery swiftly and aim to dispatch your goods within 24 hours (excluding weekends and UK public holidays), however please note that all delivery times are estimated and during busy periods dispatch may take longer.
Once your delivery has been dispatched you will receive an email from us. If your delivery is tracked you will also receive a tracking number so that you can check the progress of your delivery. To avoid any damage to your purchase some deliveries may be sent in more than one package, each may have separate delivery dates and tracking information.

Undeliverable Parcels

If all attempts to deliver your products have failed and your purchase is returned to us, we will contact you to arrange a new delivery, please note that you will be charged for redelivery.
