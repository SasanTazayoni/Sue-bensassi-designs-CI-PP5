Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Sue Bensassi Designs uses a small number of cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or analytics purposes.

3. Cookies We Set

The following cookies are set directly by this website:

Cookie Purpose Duration
sessionid Maintains your session — keeps you logged in and preserves your shopping cart between pages. Session / 2 weeks if "remember me" is used
csrftoken A security cookie that protects forms on this site against cross-site request forgery attacks. 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services used on this website may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

Stripe

We use Stripe to process payments securely. When you proceed to checkout, Stripe may set cookies on your device for fraud prevention and to ensure the security of your transaction. These cookies are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Font Awesome

This website uses Font Awesome to display icons. The Font Awesome kit script may set functional cookies to track kit usage. No personal data is collected. For more information, see the Font Awesome Privacy Policy.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies (such as sessionid and csrftoken) will affect the functionality of this website — you may not be able to log in or complete a purchase.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us via our contact page.
